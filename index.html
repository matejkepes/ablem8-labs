About

ablem8 labs is led by Matej Képeš, Head of Engineering at Pocketworks and a software engineer with over 9 years of experience building mobile and web applications.

Since 2016, Matej has worked across the full spectrum of software development—from individual contributor to engineering leadership—building products for companies across Europe. His experience spans native mobile development (iOS, Android), modern web frameworks, and backend systems.

Based in Bratislava, Slovakia, Matej provides strategic technology consulting and leadership services to companies navigating critical technical decisions, scaling their engineering teams, and optimizing their technology infrastructure.
